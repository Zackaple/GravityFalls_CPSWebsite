About us

Gravity Falls was born out of a deep reverence for the untamed wild and the absolute freedom of the great outdoors. We believe that the right equipment doesn't just help you survive the elements—it lets you truly connect with them. From backcountry peaks to quiet lakeside valleys, we are here to provide standard-setting gear for every stage of your wild journey.

We bring premium, field-tested gear to outdoor enthusiasts globally

We design and field-test every single tent, sleeping bag, and trail essential under the harshest real-world conditions to guarantee unparalleled durability. Our team consists of seasoned backpackers, survivalists, and outdoor enthusiasts who understand that out in the wild, the reliability of your gear is your greatest asset. We leave absolutely no room for compromise.

Whether you are a solo trekker braving high-altitude alpine winds or a family creating lifelong memories around a weekend campfire, CPS equips you with engineered comfort and safety. We continually refine our materials and production standards to make sure that the only thing you have to focus on out there is the majesty of the wilderness.

We empower adventurers

True exploration requires confidence. That is why our mission extends far beyond retail—we want to remove the barriers keeping people from exploring the outdoors. By focusing on intuitive setups, thermal efficiency, and lightweight configurations, we build gear that makes the wilderness approachable for beginners while remaining completely dependable for elite explorers.

We help your outdoor experiences grow safer and bigger

Every product we manufacture stands on three fundamental core pillars, ensuring that you can explore further, sleep deeper, and hike safer.

Field-Tested Gear

Proven on rugged mountain peaks and wet valley floors to withstand extreme wind, rain, and wear.

Eco-Friendly Footprint

Crafted with responsibly sourced materials and recycled fabrics to protect the very spaces we love to explore.

Global Expedition

Engineered to deliver compact portability and tactical utility, no matter where your map takes you.
